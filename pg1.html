TIPS





Tips that every one should know.

1.stay away from the storm.

2.get ever GOOD weapon you can get.

3.jump sideways over and over in different directions if someone is shooting at you.

4.if you have good weapons go to gun shots so that you can snipe the people that are already in battle.

5.if you need a med kit or a shield potion get it as soon as possible because you never know when you'll need it.

6.remember if your car needs gas you can go to a gas station.

7.if you get a water boon you can go in the water and heal, you can also heal friends if they are in the water with you

8.try to get a Typhoon blade and don't run with it unless you need to

9.if you are trying to hide don't run or shoot your gun because people can hear that.

10.you can hear people running and shooting

11.if you put a sprit near a sprit shrine and then you activate it you will get a bunch of insane loot
