PC Controls

First things first, moving your character around Fortnite's vast landscape is crucial. Use the W, A, S, D keys for movement. Looking to jump into action or over obstacles? The Spacebar is your go-to. Now, for the exciting part - building! Press Q to switch to build mode, and use the mouse wheel to select the structure you wish to build.

Spot an enemy? Aim with the right mouse button (RMB) and shoot using the left mouse button (LMB). Don't forget to reload with R. If you need to switch weapons or items quickly, the number keys (1-6) will become your best friends. And for those sneaky moments, press Ctrl to crouch.
